Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in their original packaging with a receipt. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 3-5 business days for domestic orders and 7-14 days for international. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Contact us for specific shipping rates or check our shipping calculator during checkout.

How can I track my order?

You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped. You can also track your order status directly from your account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and select digital wallets for secure transactions.
